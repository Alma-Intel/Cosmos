- organizations table (part of organizations db)

uuid (uuid, primary key) | name (str, not null) | created_at (datetime, not null) | updated_at (datetime, not null) | active (str, not null) | meta_data (json, optional)


- authorization table (part of organizations db)

uuid (uuid, primary key) | apikey (str, not null) | organization_uuid (uuid, not null) | created_at (datetime, not null) | last_used (datetime, optional) | apiKeyName (str, not null)


- messages table (part of conversations db)

uuid (uuid, primary key) | sender_uuid (uuid, not null) | conversation_uuid (uuid, not null) | content (text, not null) | type (text, not null) | link (text, optional) | channel (text, not null) | subchannel (text, optional) | created_at (datetime, not null)| updated_at (datetime, not null) | meta_data (json, optional) | origin (str, not null) | alma_internal_organization (uuid, not null)


- conversations table (part of conversations db)

uuid (uuid, primary key) | agents (list) | external_participants (list, not null) | created_at (datetime, not null) | updated_at (datetime, not null) | meta_data (json) | origin (str, not null) | alma_internal_organization (uuid, not null)


- agents db

uuid (uuid, not null) | name (str) | email (str) | phone_number (str) | other_info (json) | internal (bool, not null) |origin(str, not null) | alma_internal_organization (uuid, not null)


- analytics db

uuid (uuid, primary key) | conversation_uuid (not null, uuid) | context_start_index (int) | context_end_index (int) | analysis_type (str, not null) | result (json, not null) | origin (str, not null) | alma_internal_organization (uuid, not null) | created_at (datetime, not null)


- follow_up db

event_uuid (uuid, primary key) | conversation_uuid (uuid, not null) | agent_uuid (uuid, not null) | score (int, not null) | follow_up_date (datetime, not null)

- Events DB

uuid (uuid, primary key) | conversation_uuid (uuid, not null) | agent_uuid (uuid, not null) | created_at (datetime, not null) | scheduled_to (datetime, optional) | event_type (str, not null) | event_subtype (str, optional) | json (json, optional)
